# 🏔 Project 100 Product Specification

Version 1.0

---

# Vision

Project 100 is a calm expedition companion that helps endurance athletes focus on today's journey while quietly preparing for tomorrow's summit.

It is not a replacement for existing training platforms.

It exists alongside them.

---

# Mission

Every morning Project 100 should answer three questions.

1. Where am I on my journey?
2. What is today's expedition?
3. Why does today matter?

If it achieves those three things, it has succeeded.

---

# User Experience

The ideal interaction lasts around twenty seconds.

Open the app.

Understand today's expedition.

Close the app.

Go outside.

---

# Design Philosophy

The experience should feel like:

- Opening an expedition journal.
- Unfolding an Ordnance Survey map.
- Visiting a National Park visitor centre.

The experience should never feel like:

- Social media.
- A spreadsheet.
- Corporate software.
- A dashboard full of numbers.

---

# Success

Project 100 succeeds if it helps the athlete become more confident rather than more dependent.

Confidence is the primary product outcome.

---

# Product Goal

Help the athlete enjoy the journey one expedition at a time.
