# ADR-001

## Title

Mindset Before Metrics

---

## Status

Accepted

---

## Date

July 2026

---

## Context

Most fitness applications immediately present statistics.

Project 100 aims to create a calmer experience.

---

## Decision

The Trailhead screen should present:

Greeting

↓

Today's Expedition

↓

Thought

↓

Objective

↓

Statistics (where applicable)

---

## Consequences

The athlete begins each day with purpose rather than numbers.

Future features should respect this hierarchy.
